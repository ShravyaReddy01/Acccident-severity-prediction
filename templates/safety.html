Speed Limits

Always obey the posted speed limits. Speeding is a major cause of accidents.

Seat Belts

Wear your seat belt at all times. It's the law and it can save your life in case of an accident.

Traffic Signals

Follow traffic signals and signs. Stop at red lights, yield at yield signs, and obey other traffic signals.

Right of Way

Yield the right of way to pedestrians and other vehicles as indicated by traffic laws and signs.

No Texting and Driving

Do not text or use your phone while driving. It is dangerous and can lead to accidents.

DUI Laws

Do not drink and drive. Driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs is illegal and extremely dangerous.
